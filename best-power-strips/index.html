A modern power strip is the unsung hero of any desk, entertainment center, or workshop. Beyond simply multiplying outlets, today’s models add USB charging, surge protection, smart controls, and rotating plugs that make fitting bulky adapters a breeze. Choosing the right one means balancing outlet count, cord length, surge-joule rating, and whether you want smart-home integration, all without creating a fire hazard or wasting valuable space. Before you buy, think about where you‘ll use it. A home office might need widely spaced outlets for laptop bricks and monitor power supplies, while a nightstand could prioritize USB ports for phones and tablets. If you’re protecting expensive electronics like a TV or gaming PC, get a model with a high joule rating and a connected-equipment warranty. And if you regularly rearrange your setup, a rotating or flat-plug design can save your sanity.
